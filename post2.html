My Top 5

Here is a list of some of my top 5 favorite books and movies!

Each of these were chosen for different reasone but ultimately it comes down to a few things: entertainment level, romance, and favorite characters. You'll see each book and movie has for each category to show what specifically I enjoyed about it.

Books:

   
  1. The Harry Potter Series by JK Rowling
    • This is a fantasy, adventure series. If you have not read it, YOU HAVE TOO! I have reread this series so many times.
    • Entertainment: 10/10 (The world JK Rowling creates is amazing)
    • Romance: 4/10 (there is some relationships, but not a big focus. In the sixth book is when the characters really start developing feelings for other people and it's awkward but so funny)
    • Favorite Characters: I love Ginny in the books (her character is trashed in the movies). Hermione is also top of the list. Fred and George are hilarious so I love them.
  2. The Selection Series by Kiera cass
    • This is a dystopian, romance series. Also important note - I only loved the first three books (the last two are with other characters and I was not a fan)
    • Entertainment: 7/10 (it is basically the Bachelor combined with Hunger Games so action and excitment was definitely there)
    • Romance: 7/10 (love the relationship that develops between the two characters - it is rocky but real)
    • Favorite Characters: Prince Maxon is my favorite but I also love Marlee (a friend)
  3. The Lunar Chronicles by Marissa Meyer
    • This is a fantasy, dystopian series. Each book is about a different girl based off of a different fairytale story. All of their stories connect with each other and it is so fun to see the connections between the characters and the fairytale they are based on.
    • Entertainment: 9/10 (With each new character added, the exctiemnt, thrill, and adventure grows greater and greater)
    • Romance: 10/10 (there are 4 different relationships and each of them are beautifula dn unique in their own way)
    • Favorite Characters: Cinder and Prince Kai are my favorite (they are the cinderella fairytale twist). I also love Thorne (He is FLyn Rider in the Rapunzel story so he's the best)

Movies:

  1. Cinderella (2015)
    • This is the live action Cinderella movie Disney made and it was so well done. They did such an amazing job with the character development and adding the well-known story
    • Entertainment: 10/10 (the dancing scene is beautiful. Don't get me started on the dress transformation - literal perfection)
    • Romance: 10/10 (wow. The chemistry between Kit and Cinderella is beautiful)
    • Favorite Characters: Cinderella of course (Lily James does a magnificent job portraying her) and the Prince of course (just look at those eyes)
    • My all time favorite quote comes from this movie, it is said by Cinderella's mother "Have courage and be Kind."
  2. Crazy Rich Asians
    • This is a romcom and it is hilarious and so romantic
    • Entertainment: 7/10 (a crazy family, lots of drama, and a beautiful wedding scene)
    • Romance: 9/10 (They are so cute and the love they have for each other is so pure)
    • Favorite Characters: Rachel is so cool. Peik Lin is played by Awkwifina so her character is hilarious. Astrid is so pure and wholesome.
